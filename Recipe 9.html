Cooking with Meaning - Ginger Drink

Ingredients (makes 8 servings)

  • 1/4 cup fresh grated ginger root
  • 3 cups boiling water
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp whole cloves
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 1/4 cup fresh squeezed lime juice
  • 1/2 cup fresh squeezed orange juice
  • 4 cups cool water
  • 3 tsp honey>

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine grated ginger root with 3 cups boiling water.
  2. Add sugar, whole cloves, and cinnamon sticks. Stir well, lower heat, and simmer for 25 minutes.
  3. Strain through a fine mesh strainer, pressing ginger to extract liquid. Discard solids.
  4. Add lime juice, orange juice, and 4 cups cool water.
  5. Strain again, avoiding sediment at the bottom.
  6. Serve hot or refridgerate.
