Cooking with Meaning - Orange Chicken

Ingredients (makes 4 servings)

  • 2 pounds Skinless Chicken Breasts (boneless, cut in 1-inch cubes)
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 tablespoon Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 2 cups Orange Juice
  • ¼ cup Coconut Aminos
  • 3 tablespoons Honey
  • 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • ½ teaspoon Black Pepper
  • ½ tablespoon Fresh Ginger (grated)
  • ½ tablespoon Orange Zest
  • 4 cups Steamed Broccoli (or green beans)
  • 2 cups Rice (cooked)

Procedure

  1. Cook green beans and rice according to package directions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a sauté pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook 10 minutes until browned.
  3. Mix all sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  4. Remove cooked chicken and set aside.
  5. Pour sauce into the same pan.
  6. Boil 15–20 minutes, stirring often, until thickened.
  7. Add chicken back into the sauce and coat well.
  8. Serve over steamed vegetables and rice.

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

  • Calories: ~520
  • Protein: ~45 g
  • Carbohydrates: ~55 g (Sugars ~28 g)
  • Fat: ~14 g (Saturated Fat ~2 g)
  • Fiber: ~3 g
  • Sodium: ~520 mg
  • Potassium: ~750 mg
