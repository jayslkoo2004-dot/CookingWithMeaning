Cooking with Meaning - Chicken Pasta

Ingredients (makes 6 servings)

  • 1–1½ pounds Chicken Breast, boneless & skinless, cut in half lengthwise
  • 2 teaspoons Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Dried Oregano
  • 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • ½ teaspoon Dried Rosemary
  • ½ teaspoon Dried Thyme
  • ¼ teaspoon Black Pepper
  • Pinch Crushed Red Pepper (optional)
  • 3 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1 Small Onion, diced
  • 5–6 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 12 ounces pasta, cooked al dente
  • Salt for pasta water
  • 1–2 tablespoons Basil leaves
  • Parmesan Cheese (optional)

Procedure

  1. Cut chicken in half lengthwise to create thinner pieces.
  2. Mix salt, oregano, garlic powder, rosemary, thyme, black pepper, and crushed red pepper.
  3. Season chicken on both sides with half of the seasoning mix.
  4. Heat olive oil in a deep skillet over medium heat.
  5. Cook chicken 5 minutes per side, or until internal temperature reaches 165°F. Remove and keep warm.
  6. Add onions to the same skillet and cook 2–3 minutes.
  7. Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
  8. Add crushed tomatoes and remaining seasoning mix. Stir and bring to a gentle simmer.
  9. Simmer sauce 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  10. Cook pasta according to instructions. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
  11. Slice cooked chicken into strips or cubes.
  12. Taste sauce and adjust seasoning if needed.
  13. Add pasta to sauce with 2–3 tablespoons pasta water. Toss to coat.
  14. Add chicken and basil. Toss until combined.
  15. Serve with parmesan and extra crushed red pepper if desired.
