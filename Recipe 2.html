Cooking with Meaning - Banana Bread

Ingredients (makes 1 serving)

  • ⅓ cup (75 g) melted coconut oil, olive oil, or vegetable oil
  • ½ cup honey (168 g) or maple syrup (155 g)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup (225 g) mashed ripe bananas (2 ½ medium or 2 large)
  • ¼ cup (56 g) milk of choice or water
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon (plus more for topping)
  • 1 ¾ cups (220 g) white whole wheat flour or whole wheat flour
  • Optional: ½ cup mix-ins (nuts, chocolate chips, dried fruit, banana slices)

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C) and grease a 9×5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Whisk together the oil and honey in a large bowl. Add eggs and whisk well.
  3. Mix in mashed bananas and milk.
  4. If coconut oil solidifies, warm the bowl briefly until melted.
  5. Add baking soda, vanilla, salt, and cinnamon. Whisk to combine.
  6. Stir in flour until just combined. Some lumps are fine.
  7. Fold in optional mix-ins.
  8. Pour batter into loaf pan. Sprinkle cinnamon and swirl with a knife if desired.
  9. Bake 55–60 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  10. Cool 10 minutes in the pan, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Nutrition Facts

Based on 12 slices:

  • Calories: ~210
  • Carbohydrates: ~32 g
  • Sugars: ~15 g
  • Protein: ~3.5 g

  • Fiber: ~3 g
  • Sodium: ~180 mg
