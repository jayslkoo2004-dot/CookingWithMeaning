Cooking with Meaning - Baked Salmon

Ingredients (makes 4 servings)

  • 1 1/2 lb salmon fillet, cut into 4 filets
  • 2 Tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 2 Tbsp extra light olive oil or vegetable oil
  • 2 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced, pressed, or grated
  • 1/2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp freshly cracked black pepper
  • 1/2 lemon, sliced into 4 rings (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F and line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment, foil, or a silicone liner.
  2. Arrange salmon fillets on the baking sheet, skin-side down.
  3. In a small bowl, combine parsley, garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper.
  4. Brush the marinade generously over the tops and sides of the salmon.
  5. Place one lemon slice on each piece.
  6. Bake uncovered for 12–15 minutes, or until flaky and the internal temperature reaches 145°F at the thickest part.
  7. Do not overcook. Serve warm.
