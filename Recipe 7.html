Cooking with Meaning - Avocado Toast with Egg

Ingredients (makes 2 servings)

  • 2 slices whole‑grain or sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs (sunny‑side up)
  • 2 strips crispy bacon
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread until crisp.
  2. For extra flavor, grill the bread with olive oil in a pan.
  3. Remove the avocado pit and mash with a fork.
  4. Add a pinch of salt, black pepper, and lemon juice for freshness.
  5. Fry bacon in a pan until crispy, then drain on a paper towel.
  6. Sunny‑side egg method:
    1. Spray a small non‑stick skillet with cooking spray.
    2. Crack an egg into a small dish without breaking the yolk.
    3. Heat skillet over medium until very hot.
    4. Gently slip the egg into the pan and reduce heat to medium.
    5. Cook 2 minutes until whites are opaque; cover briefly if needed.
    6. Slide egg out carefully without breaking the yolk.
  7. Spread mashed avocado over the toasted bread.
  8. Place crispy bacon on top.
  9. Add the cooked egg and sprinkle with red pepper flakes.
