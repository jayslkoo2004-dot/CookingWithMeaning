Cooking with Meaning - Caesar Salad

Dressing Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise (Hellman's or S&W Whole Egg)
  • 1/2 tsp garlic, finely minced
  • 2 anchovy fillets or 3/4 tsp anchovy paste
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese (or 1/3 cup store‑bought grated)
  • 3–4 tbsp milk (to adjust consistency)
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Garlic Crouton Ingredients

  • 2–3 slices white bread (about 2/3" thick)
  • 1 garlic clove, cut in half
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Salad Ingredients

  • 150g / 5 oz streaky bacon, cooked and chopped
  • 10 cups romaine (cos) lettuce, chopped, washed & dried
  • Freshly grated parmesan, for garnish

Optional Extras — Chicken & Eggs

  • 2–4 eggs, cooked to your preference, peeled and halved
  • 500g / 1 lb chicken breast fillets (2 pieces)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions (makes 5-6 servings)

Dressing

  1. Blend all dressing ingredients in a food processor, starting with 3 tbsp milk.
  2. Adjust salt, pepper, and milk for consistency.
  3. Let rest 20 minutes to develop flavor.

Bacon

  1. Place bacon in a cold skillet and turn heat to medium‑high.
  2. Cook until golden on both sides, then drain and chop.

Garlic Croutons

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C / 350°F.
  2. Toast bread lightly until surface is dry.
  3. Rub both sides with cut garlic.
  4. Cut into cubes, drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle salt, and bake until golden.

Assemble

  1. Toss lettuce with half the dressing; taste and add more if desired.
  2. Transfer to serving bowl.
  3. Top with bacon, croutons, and parmesan.
  4. Add chicken and eggs if using.

Optional — Chicken

  1. Pound chicken to even thickness or slice horizontally.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook in bacon fat: 5 minutes first side, 2 minutes second side.
  4. Rest 5 minutes, then slice.

Optional — Eggs

  1. Simmer eggs 3 minutes (soft), 4 minutes (medium), or 6 minutes (hard).
  2. Cool in cold water, peel, and halve.

Nutrition Facts

Based on 6 servings, without chicken & eggs:

  • Calories: ~420
  • Protein: ~9 g
  • Carbohydrates: ~10 g
  • Sugars: ~3 g
  • Fat: ~38 g
  • Saturated Fat: ~8 g
  • Fiber: ~2 g
  • Sodium: ~720 mg

With chicken & eggs: add ~200–250 calories and +30 g protein.
