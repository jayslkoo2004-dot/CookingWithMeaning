Cooking with Meaning - Soba Noodles

Ingredients (makes 4 servings)

  • 10 ounces soba noodles (dry)
  • 3 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil or peanut oil
  • 1/4 cup green onions, thinly sliced

Garnish

  • Green onions
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Cook soba noodles in boiling water according to package instructions. Rinse under cold water, drain, and set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together rice wine vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add canola or peanut oil and heat until shimmering.
  4. Add sliced green onions and cook for about 30 seconds until softened.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce mixture and cook for 30 seconds.
  6. Add the noodles and toss until heated through and coated.
  7. Garnish with sesame seeds and additional green onions.
